The Rules

1. LOOK for a daily text which will name the sea creature of the day.

2. DRAW the subject. Any form, medium and interpretation is acceptable.

3. TEXT me a photo of your bird creation and I’ll upload all photos to a website each night - a fun way to see everyone’s different styles grouped together!

4. HELP? A link to a random daily drawing tutorial will be posted simply because I need drawing help and inspiration. No need to use it. Found a cool resource? Please send it my way to share it!

5. CONSISTENCY is key! It is more important than technique, perfection, and maybe even improvement.

6. FLEX your creativity muscle and challenge yourself to find a few minutes every day to show up for yourself and the group!
